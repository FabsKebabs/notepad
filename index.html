Help & Support

How to Use

Converting Handwriting to Text

  1. On the Home page, use your finger or stylus to write on the canvas.
  2. Tap "Convert to Text" to recognize your handwriting.
  3. Your converted text will appear in the text display area.
  4. Use "Clear Canvas" to start over or "Clear Text" to clear the converted text.

Drawing Studio

  1. Upload an image to draw on by tapping "Upload Image".
  2. Adjust image opacity using the slider.
  3. Draw on the image with your selected color and thickness.
  4. Save your creation using the "Save Drawing" button.

Tips for Better Recognition

  • Write clearly with adequate spacing between words.
  • Use a darker color for better contrast.
  • Write at a medium size ‚Äì not too small or too large.
  • Avoid cursive writing for better recognition accuracy.
  • Keep the canvas clean by using the "Clear Canvas" button between conversions.

FAQ

Why is my handwriting not being recognized correctly?

Recognition accuracy depends on handwriting clarity, lighting, and contrast. Try using a darker color and writing more clearly.

Can I save my converted text?

Yes, your converted text is automatically saved in the History section. You can also manually save important texts to the "My Texts" section.

How do I change the app theme?

You can toggle between light and dark modes in the menu sidebar or in the Settings page.

Does this app work offline?

The drawing features work offline, but text recognition requires an internet connection for the best accuracy.
